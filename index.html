About Neil

I am a web developer. I spend my whole day, practically every day, experimenting with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript; and inhaling a wide variety of potentially useful information through a few hundred RSS feeds. I build websites that delight and inform. I am curious, and I enjoy projects that challenge me to learn something new and stretch in a different direction. I do my best to stay on top of changes in the state of the art so that I can meet challenges with tools well suited to the job at hand. The list of projects I follow on Twitter will give you a good idea of the types of tools I prefer to be using, and my list will give you a glimpse into the reading material I find interesting enough to share.
